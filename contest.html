Submission Guidelines

Theme & Originality

  • **Theme:** Your artwork must be **pet-related** or inspired by the world of Pets Unplugged. Feel free to get creative with any animal you love!
  • **Originality:** Artwork must be **100% original** and created by the submitter.
  • **Restrictions:** No tracing, copying, or using copyrighted material (other than your own) is allowed.
  • **Format:** Submissions can be **physical drawings or digital art**. Please send a high-quality, clear scan or photograph of your artwork.

How to Submit

  • **Submission Method:** Email your entry to: **contest@petsunplugged.com**
  • **Subject Line:** Must be **Monthly Art Contest Entry**
  • **Include in Email:** Your Name (or Guardian's Name if under 18), your age (optional), and the title of your artwork.
  • **Review Period:** Submissions are accepted continuously, and we will review all entries received in the preceding 30-day period before announcing a winner.
