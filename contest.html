🎨 Monthly Pet Art Contest: Show Your Talent!

Draw your favorite pet and submit for a chance to win! Open to all ages — a new winner is chosen approximately every 30 days.

🏆 New winner chosen approximately every 30 days!

Submission Guidelines

Theme & Originality

  • Theme: Your artwork must be pet-related or inspired by the world of Pets Unplugged. Any animal welcome!
  • Originality: Artwork must be 100% original and created by the submitter.
  • Restrictions: No tracing, copying, or using copyrighted material (other than your own).
  • Format: Physical drawings or digital art accepted. Please submit a high-quality, clear scan or photograph.

How to Submit

  • Submission Method: Use the form below, or email your entry to: contest@petsunplugged.com
  • Subject Line: Must be Monthly Art Contest Entry
  • Include: Your Name (or Guardian's Name if under 18), your age (optional), and the title of your artwork.
  • Review Period: Submissions accepted continuously; all entries in the preceding 30-day period are reviewed before announcing a winner.
  • Required: Agreement to transfer exclusive rights to your artwork to Pets Unplugged, including the right to use it on merchandise.

🏆 What the Winner Gets

One winning artist is selected approximately every 30 days to receive all of the following:

Website & Social Media Feature

A featured post on the official Pets Unplugged website and social media platforms.

Official Contest Winner Showcase

Your winning illustration will be showcased as the official monthly contest winner.

Custom Hoodie with Your Art

A custom-made, high-quality hoodie featuring your winning artwork printed on it.

Exclusive QR Animation of Your Art

Your winning artwork gets animated — you'll receive a unique QR code linking to a 6-second animation of your illustration!

Selection & Contact

Winners are selected by the Pets Unplugged team based on creativity, originality, and artistic skill. The team's decision is final.

Winners will be contacted via the email address used for submission. You must respond within 7 days to claim your prize, or an alternate winner may be chosen.

*By submitting your artwork, you grant Pets Unplugged the exclusive, perpetual, and transferable right to use, reproduce, display, publish, modify, and sell your submitted artwork on our website, social media, promotional materials, and on any merchandise or product for commercial sale, with credit to you as the artist.*
