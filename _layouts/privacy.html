Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

1. Who We Are

Pets Unplugged is a sole proprietorship operating at petsunplugged.com. We create interactive pet coloring books with QR code animations. For privacy-related questions, contact us at hello@petsunplugged.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to operate our website and serve you:

  • Email address and name — when you sign up for our newsletter via Brevo (Sendinblue)
  • Contest submission data — name, email address, age (optional), artwork title, and uploaded artwork file when you enter our monthly art contest via FormSubmit
  • Usage data — pages visited, time on site, browser type, and general location (country/region) collected automatically via Google Analytics

We do not collect or store payment information. All purchases are processed securely by Amazon (physical book) or Payhip (digital PDF), and we never have access to your payment card details.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Email address — to send you newsletters, free sample pages, product updates, and exclusive discounts that you signed up for. You can unsubscribe at any time via the link in any email.
  • Contest submissions — to evaluate artwork, contact winners, and (with your consent via the submission form) feature winning artwork on our website and social media.
  • Analytics data — to understand how visitors use our site so we can improve it. This data is aggregated and anonymous.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services that may process your data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Google Analytics — website traffic analysis. Google Privacy Policy
  • Brevo (Sendinblue) — email newsletter delivery. Brevo Privacy Policy
  • FormSubmit — contest form submission processing. FormSubmit Privacy Policy
  • Amazon — physical book sales and payment processing. Amazon Privacy Policy
  • Payhip — digital PDF sales and payment processing. Payhip Privacy Policy
  • Vimeo — video hosting for QR code animations. Vimeo Privacy Policy

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies primarily through Google Analytics to understand site usage. These cookies do not personally identify you. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time, though this may affect some site functionality.

6. Children's Privacy

Our products are designed for all ages, including children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent. Contest submissions from artists under 18 must include a parent or guardian's name and contact information. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us at hello@petsunplugged.com and we will promptly delete it.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct any inaccurate data
  • Delete your data (right to be forgotten)
  • Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time
  • Opt out of Google Analytics via the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@petsunplugged.com.

8. Data Retention

We retain your email address for as long as you remain subscribed to our newsletter. Contest submission data is retained for the duration of the contest period and up to 12 months afterward. You may request deletion at any time.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
hello@petsunplugged.com
