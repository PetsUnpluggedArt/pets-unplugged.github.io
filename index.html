About Pets Unplugged

Pets Unplugged was born from a passion for art, animals, and the simple joy of coloring. We believe in taking a moment to disconnect from the digital world and immerse ourselves in creativity. Our hand-drawn illustrations are designed to spark imagination and bring a smile to your face, especially now that every page offers a quick, delightful animated reward!

Every coloring book and piece of merchandise is crafted with love, aiming to share the therapeutic and joyful experience of bringing vibrant life to our furry, feathered, and finned friends. Thank you for joining our journey!

Frequently Asked Questions

How do the animated QR codes work?

Each illustration has a unique QR code printed next to it. Simply scan the code using your smartphone camera (or a QR reader app). It will open a short, 6-second animated clip showing the original, fully colored illustration subtly moving, adding a magical "life" element to the artwork you just colored!

What kind of illustrations are in your coloring books?

Our books feature a diverse range of hand-drawn pet illustrations, from fluffy puppies and playful kittens to exotic birds and aquatic wonders. Each illustration is designed with varying levels of detail to appeal to both beginners and experienced colorists.

Can I buy digital copies of the coloring books?

Yes! Many of our coloring books are available as instant digital downloads in PDF format. This allows you to print your favorite pages at home as many times as you like. Look for the "Digital Download" section on our website.

What kind of merchandise do you offer?

We offer a growing range of merchandise, including mugs, t-shirts, tote bags, and sticker packs, all featuring unique illustrations from our coloring books. We're always adding new items, so check back often!

How can I stay updated on new releases?

The best way to stay in the loop is to sign up for our newsletter in the "Contact" section. You'll receive exclusive updates on new book launches, merchandise, and special offers directly to your inbox!
