Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Pets Unplugged an interactive coloring book?

Each page contains a hand-drawn pet illustration and a unique QR code. After you color the page, scan the QR code with any smartphone camera — no app needed — and watch a 6-second animation of your pet illustration play on screen.

What age group are these interactive coloring books for?

Our interactive coloring books are designed for all ages — the illustrations are simple enough for young children but detailed enough for teens and adults to enjoy. They make great gifts for dog lovers, families, and classrooms.

Do I need to download an app to see the animations?

No app required! Simply open your phone's camera, point it at the QR code on any completed page, and the animation will open in your browser instantly.
