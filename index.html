Frequently Asked Questions

How do the scannable animations work?

Next to every illustration in the book, you'll find a small, unique QR code. Simply point your smartphone camera at the code (no special app needed!), and a link will pop up, taking you to a 6-second animation of that specific illustration.

Can I see the animations on my computer?

Yes, while the QR codes are designed for easy mobile access, you can manually type the URL (which appears when you scan the code) into any desktop web browser to view the animations.

What age range are the coloring books for?

Our books are perfect for all ages, from older children (8+) to adults. The illustrations feature a mix of simple and complex designs to engage any coloring enthusiast.
