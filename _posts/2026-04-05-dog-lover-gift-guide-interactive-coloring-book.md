## Other Great Gifts for Dog Lovers (And How They Compare)

To be fair, here's an honest look at the full gift landscape for dog lovers:

![Flat lay of the PAWS interactive coloring book open to a completed dog illustration alongside colored pencils and a wrapped gift](/assets/images/gift-comparison-flatlay.webp)

**Custom pet portrait** — Highly personal and beautiful, but expensive ($50–$200+) and takes weeks to arrive. Better for milestone gifts like birthdays or anniversaries.

**Dog breed jewelry** — Thoughtful if you know the exact breed, and a classic choice. Not particularly interactive or experiential, but always appreciated.

**Dog subscription box** — Fun and recurring, but primarily for the dog rather than the owner. Great if you want to spoil both.

**Personalized dog book** — Books that feature the recipient's dog's name are clever but often feel a bit gimmicky. Novelty fades quickly.

**Interactive coloring book** — Unique, creative, screen-free, works for all ages, available in two price points, and the QR animation is a genuine surprise moment. The experience lives on every time they sit down to color.
